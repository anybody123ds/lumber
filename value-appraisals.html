Properly Calculated Timber Value Appraisals

Whether you are determining a tax basis or preparing for a land transaction, Stillwater Forestry is here to help you with your timber appraisal. In summary, an appraisal is performed to determine the volume as well as the value of the timber found on a parcel of land.

We utilize a statistically based process called a timber cruise to determine the volume. After that, we calculate the value based on timber prices from recently completed harvests. You can expect to receive a condensed report that contains all the numbers you need.

Timber Value Appraisals

As a landowner, making informed decisions is essential to business success. To ensure this, you should treat your timberland as a valuable asset by having it appraised. Here are some proven benefits of getting a timber appraisal:

Measuring Long-Term Progress

An effective way of measuring the progress of your forest management strategy is by getting an inventory appraisal at the time of harvest as well as every decade that passes afterward. This allows you to determine if the volume and value of your timber increase every year.

Timber Value Appraisal

Whenever you acquire land, having an appraisal provides you with a benchmark figure. This figure serves as the basis of depleting income that is generated from timber harvests.

Boost Your Property Value

Getting an appraisal essentially gives the standing timber in your land a monetary value. This will definitely increase the value of your property, allowing you to get more money out of a sale.

When it comes to timber appraisal, Stillwater Forestry never takes any shortcuts. We go through a series of extensive steps to ensure that the numbers we present to you are nothing short of accurate. These include:
